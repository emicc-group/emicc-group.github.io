Pei Zhi

Associate Professor of Industrial Engineering

Zhejiang University of Technology, College of Mechanical Engineering

Biography

Dr. Zhi Pei, Associate Professor, Institute of Industrial Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology. From 2001 to 2011, he received his BACHELOR of Engineering and Doctor of Engineering degree in Management Science and Engineering in the Department of Industrial Engineering, Tsinghua University. He has been teaching in Zhejiang University of Technology since July 2011. From 2015 to 2016, he was a visiting student at North Carolina State University.

He has won the special prize of Science and Technology Award of China Chamber of Commerce, the second prize of Wu Wenjun Artificial Intelligence Science and Technology Award, and the third prize of China Machinery Industry Science and Technology Award. He has hosted 3 national Natural Science Foundation projects, 3 Zhejiang Provincial Natural Science Foundation projects, and participated in a number of national and Zhejiang Provincial Natural Science Foundation projects, as well as a number of enterprise cooperation projects.

He published more than 30 SCI/EI retrieval papers, including 1 ESI highly cited paper. He is currently a member of IEEE, a reviewer of Mathematical Reviews (149310), a member of The Production System Professional Committee of the National Society of Mechanical Engineering, a Member of the Behavioral Operations Research and Management Branch of the Operations Research Society of China, and a project review expert of the National Natural Science Foundation of China. Graduate dissertation evaluation expert of Academic Degree Center of Ministry of Education, director of Computer Integrated Manufacturing System, member of graduate Dissertation Guidance and Evaluation Committee of Industrial Engineering department of Tsinghua University. His Research achievements have been published in Annals of Operations Research, International Journal of Production Research, IEEE Transactions on Automation International academic journals such as Science and Engineering, Computers & Industrial Engineering and Information Sciences.
