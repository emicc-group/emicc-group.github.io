Yi Wenchao

Associate Professor, Master Instructor

Zhejiang University of Technology, College of Mechanical Engineering

Biography

Dr. Wenchao Yi, Associate Professor, Institute of Industrial Engineering. In 2016-2017, she went to Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, for Postdoctoral research. And She joined the Department of Industrial Engineering and Logistics, School of Mechanical Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology from 2018.

Educations

  • Postdoc. at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, 2016-2017

  • Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, 2011-2016

  • B.S. in Industrial Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, 2007-2011

Research Interests

  • Differential Evolution
  • Intelligent Algorithms
  • Intelligent Manufacturing

Courses

  • Intelligent optimization algorithm
