Biography

Dr. Chen Yong, Professor, Institute of Industrial Engineering. He was born in Changsha, Hunan province. He is member of Zhejiang Intelligent Manufacturing Expert Committee, standing Director and deputy secretary general of Logistics Engineering Branch of Zhejiang Mechanical Engineering Society, Deputy director of Zhejiang Automobile air conditioning industry Technology Innovation Strategic Alliance, senior lecturer of Foxconn Group.

As a consultant, for Foxconn Group, Midea Group, Wanxiang Group, Haitian Group, Hangang Group, Hangfu Group, Sinopec BP, Hengdian Group, Hangzhou Bosch Electric and other more than 20 enterprises to carry out digital and intelligent manufacturing consulting work, has rich engineering practice experience.

He published more than 50 academic papers, 7 authorized invention patents, 40 software Copyrights; He has won many honorary titles, such as my favorite teacher of Zhejiang University of Technology, my good tutor of Graduate students of Zhejiang University of Technology, E-way Mentor of Zhejiang University of Technology, and Outstanding Award of New Media Student Work of Zhejiang University of Technology.
