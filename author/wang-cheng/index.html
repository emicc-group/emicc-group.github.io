Wang Cheng

Associate Professor, Master Instructor

Zhejiang University of Technology, College of Mechanical Engineering

Biography

Dr. Cheng Wang, Associate Professor, Institute of Industrial Engineering. He is Deputy Dean of Taizhou Research Institute, Zhejiang University of Technology. He is also a teacher of the Collegel of Mechanical Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology. He has been engaged in the practice, teaching and research of Industrial Engineering and Systems Engineering for a long time.

Educations

  • Ph.D. in System Engineering, Tianjin University, China, 2007-2010

  • M.Sc. in System Engineering, Tianjin University, China, 2004-2007

Research Interests

  • Production and Service Operation Management
  • Modeling, Simulation and Optimization of Complex Systems
  • Intelligent Optimization Algorithm
  • Game Theory and Mechanism Design

Courses

  • System Engineering
  • Applied Statistics
  • Genetic Algorithm

Projects

  • 网络化环境下服务供需主体博弈分析及服务资源优化配置(国家自然科学青年基金)
  • 考虑顾客失约行为的服务预约最优提醒策略研究(浙江省自然科学基金)
