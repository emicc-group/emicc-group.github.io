Ji Zuzhen

Assistant Professor

Zhejiang University of Technology, College of Mechanical Engineering

Biography

Dr. Zuzhen Ji, Committed to related research in the direction of Industry 4.0, including industrial engineering, safety engineering and safety management, occupational health and safety, risk management, cyber-physical systems, intelligent manufacturing, system modeling and simulation, virtual reality, etc., and has published a total of SCI , more than 10 articles in EI international journals. From 2020 to 2022, he was engaged in chemical safety risk analysis and assessment research at Zhejiang University; in 2016, he was invited to join the New Zealand Institute of Safety Engineers and the New Zealand Institute of Engineers, and shared academic achievements as a speaker many times. From 2016 to 2020, he worked as an assistant lecturer at the University of Canterbury, New Zealand, responsible for the teaching of risk analysis, safety management, production management and other subjects.

Educations

  • Postdoc. at Zhejiang University, 2020-2022

  • Ph.D. University of Canterbury, New Zealand, 2016-2020

  • M.S. Brunel University, UK, 2013-2014

Research Interests

  • System simulation and optimization
  • VR simulation

  • Industrial engineering
