Lv Ruifeng

Assistant Professor of Industrial Engineering

Zhejiang University of Technology, College of Mechanical Engineering

Biography

Dr. Lv Ruifeng, assistant professor, Institute of Industrial Engineering Research, Zhejiang University of Technology. He received his Ph.D. from Harbin Institute of Technology and hee mainly engaged in horizontal subject research. Has participated in or presided over more than 20 enterprise projects information transformation and enterprise management improvement projects.

Educations

  • Ph.D. in Mechatronic Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology, China, 2003-2008
  • M.Sc. in Mechatronic Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology, China, 2001-2003
  • B.E. in Mechanical Design and Automation, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry, China, 1997-2001

Research Interests

  • Enterprise Lean Layout and Planning
  • Production Planning
  • Quality Management
  • Equipment Management

Courses

  • Facility Planning and Logistics Analysis

Projects

  • 企业委托项目，长兴博泰电子科技有限公司生产线 SK 系列结 构及工艺设计
